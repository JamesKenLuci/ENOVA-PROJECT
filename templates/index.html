About Enova Hiraya

Enova Hiraya means "New Vision, Great Imagination." We are a modern event planning service dedicated to providing a seamless, digital-first experience for managing complex celebrations. We serve as your central hub for consultation, booking, scheduling, and project tracking.

How It Works:

  1. Consultation & Booking: Use our simple 'Book Now' form to secure your initial planning session and share your event vision.
  2. Digital Dashboard: Once booked, gain access to your personalized client dashboard to track budget, schedules, and vendor communications in real-time.
  3. Flawless Execution: Our team takes the digital plan and transforms it into a stunning, real-world event, managed with precision and passion.
